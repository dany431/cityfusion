Privacy Policy

Cityfusion respects your right to privacy. We abide by the following principles and policies for the Personally Identifiable Information we collect about you through our websites ( "Site"), our mobile applications ( "Application"), emails, and other offerings (collectively "Services").

Information we collect
We collect information to provide you with the best possible experience with our Services and for the purposes described in this Policy. We collect information directly from you, about you from third parties, and automatically through your use of our Services:
  • Information you give us.
    • In order to access certain features of our Services such as posting content, demanding an event, subscribing to email newsletters, and other features, we require you to register with us. When you register with us, we require you to provide your email address, year of birth, location and gender. In addition, at your option, you may provide your name, photograph, interests and whatever biographical information you may choose to share.
    • To get the most out of the personalization and recommendation features we offer, you might indicate to us information like your favorite events, artists and venues.
  • Information we collect from Facebook and other third party services.
    • You may sign into our Site through Facebook or other third party services. If you do so, we will request your permission to access and collect information including your (i) public profile, list of friends, email address, birthday, interests, current city, personal description, publish stream, event rsvp's and likes; and (ii) to post to your friends on your behalf.
    • With your permission, we may collect data from services you use such as Spotify, Last.fm, iTunes and others in order to learn more about your entertainment preferences and tailor the content we present to you.
    • We store the information that we receive from third party services with other information that we collect from you or receive about you. We have no control over third party services use or disclose the personal information they collect about you. Please review their policies if you have questions about such disclosure.
  • Information we collect and store automatically from your use of our Services and may combine with your personal information. We automatically collect information about your use of our Services including the content you view, the things you click and other actions that you take.

    • Device and server log information.
      • usage information about the services you use and how you use them, like when you view, search and interact with our content
      • device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version and unique device identifiers, as permitted)
      • IP address
      • browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and referral URL
      • cookies that may uniquely identify your browser or your Cityfusion account

    • Location information. When you use a location-enabled Cityfusion Service, such as our mobile applications, we will request your permission to collect and process information about your actual location, like latitude and longitude provided by the location services of your mobile device. We may also use various technologies to determine location, such as using your IP address to look up geographic information. We collect this and other information using the following technologies:

    • Local storage / Flash. We may use local shared objects, also known as Flash cookies, to store your Site preferences, to personalize your visit, or to display content based upon what you view on our Site. Third Parties with whom we partner to provide certain features on our Site or to display advertising based upon your Web browsing activity, may use Flash cookies to collect and store information. We do not have access to or control over these third party cookies nor does this Privacy Policy cover such third parties' use of data. Flash LSOs are different from browser cookies because of the amount and type of data stored. Typically, you cannot typically control, delete, or disable the acceptance of Flash cookies through your web browser. For more information on Flash LSOs, or to learn how to manage your settings for Flash LSOs, go to the Adobe Flash Player Help Page, choose "Global Storage Settings Panel." We may collect and store information locally on your device using mechanisms such as browser web storage (including HTML 5) and application data caches.

    • Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs). We or the third parties we engage to track and analyze Site Data may employ clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs) to help us better manage content on the Site. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on your computer's hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on Web pages. We do not tie the information gathered by clear gifs to our customers' personally identifiable information. In addition, emails we send may contain a "web beacon" which allows us to understand the time and date of when you open an email and when you utilized a link within the email. Our web beacons do not collect Personally Identifiable Information. Users wishing to disable our web beacons should do so by turning images "off" in their email client (e.g., Outlook, Outlook Express). Please see your email provider for more information.

    • Cookies and anonymous identifiers. We use various technologies to collect and store information when you use an Cityfusion Service, this may include sending one or more cookies or anonymous identifiers to your device. We also use cookies and anonymous identifiers when you interact with services we offer to our partners, such as Cityfusion features that may appear on other sites. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but if you prefer, you can edit your browser options to block them in the future. The Help portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your computer from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. Users of our Service who disable cookies will be able to use certain features of the Service, but some features may not function including personalization and content recommendations.

    • Third Party Cookies. We, or a data provider we have engaged, may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser to enable you to receive customized ads, content or services. The cookies may reflect de-identified demographic or other data linked to data you voluntarily have submitted to us, e.g., your email address, that we may share with a third party solely in hashed, non-human readable form. You may opt out of such sharing by signing in to Cityfusion online and managing your preferences.
