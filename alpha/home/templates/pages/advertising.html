Advertising

Featured Events

These types of ads display across the top of the home page. These are designed to catch the attention of the user towards your event. They show up randomly with each new page and can be browsed through by clicking the arrow in the Featured Events box. They also show up in the reminder and "in the loop" emails. They cost $2.00 per day. You can find the option to make an event featured in the quick access box on the event itself or in your account info page under the Events Tab.

Cost per Mile (CPM)

Also known as cost per thousand. These ads you pay for a certain number of impressions. How this work is: for every 1000 impressions you would pay $ .50 to $3.00 depending on the size of the ad. So if you chose to spend $1000.00, your advertisement would be displayed 500,000 times across the screens of the viewers (based on .50 ad). This can be found in your account page titled "Create New Ad". A reminder will be emailed to you when your funds are getting low.
